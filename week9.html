Labels can disappear. Love does not.

Week 9 of 12

Week 9

Beyond
Comparison

Coffee Moment

☕

Look at the logo on your cup or the brand on the bag. You often identify coffee by the label, but underneath it is still a bean that has been transformed. How much of your identity depends on labels like job title, productivity, status, or reputation?

“I have been crucified with Christ. It is no longer I who live, but Christ who lives in me...”
~ Galatians 2:20 (ESV)

Reflection

You are living in a generation trained to brand itself. It is easy to curate an identity like a product and quietly believe that your worth rises or falls with your performance. But when work gets hard or your social standing shifts, a branded identity collapses fast. Your deepest identity is not found in what you produce but in your position as someone loved by God before you achieve a single thing. When you walk into a room, you are not there to earn worth. You are there to share the worth you already have.

Discussion Questions

  • If your job title disappeared tomorrow, how would you describe yourself to a stranger?
  • Where do you still slip from being into proving?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to loosen your grip on labels that cannot hold you and to deepen your confidence in an identity that does not depend on performance.

Personal Practice

Spend 10 minutes in total silence today. No phone, no music, no doing. Practice being in God's presence.
