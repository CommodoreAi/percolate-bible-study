Strength in
Numbers

Coffee Moment

☕

Notice the people around you and be thankful for shared relationships.

“Therefore encourage one another and build one another up, just as you are doing.”
~ 1 Thessalonians 5:11 (ESV)

Reflection

We often assume that living independently means handling everything on our own. Independence can seem safer and less vulnerable. But growth and encouragement happen with other people, because we are reminded that we are not the only ones navigating challenges. Consistent gathering builds trust, and trust creates space for honesty and support. The Lord uses our relationships to build one another up. Strength in numbers isn't about crowd size; it's about connection.

Discussion Questions

  • Why is it important to gather with others?
  • What makes you feel welcome in a group?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you when you resist social opportunities.

Personal Practice

Choose to visit a social place or gathering this week.
