“...Love your neighbor as yourself...”
~ Mark 12:31 (ESV)

Reflection

We demonstrate love by actively engaging with others in everyday situations. Love is a practice that grows with consistency, with faithful caring for others. Continue to practice building relationships you have formed in recent weeks. You will find opportunities to build into others and share what you have learned in the process. Growing together is a fantastic experience, and the Lord will continue to work in your life through loving, embodied presence.

Discussion Questions

  • How has this group changed you?
  • How will you practice presence moving forward?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you how to continue making relationships a priority in your life.

Personal Practice

Commit to continuing to build ongoing relationships.
