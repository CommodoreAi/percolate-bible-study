Coffee Moment

☕

Look at your empty or near-empty cup. The ritual is almost over, but the caffeine is now in your system, giving you energy for what comes next. Coffee is not meant to stay in the cup, and faith is not meant to stay in the study group. You have been filled so you can pour into others.

“You are the light of the world... let your light shine before others.”
~ Matthew 5:14-16 (ESV)

Reflection

The loneliness epidemic will not be solved by better apps or smarter articles. It will be changed by people who have learned how to be present. That is the outward mission now. You are not leaving this table to go back unchanged into the grind. You are going as someone who knows how to listen, how to wait, and how to build a bridge. Community is a renewable resource: the more you practice giving it, the more you discover there is to give. Carry the slow-pour philosophy into your fast world, and become the aroma of grace in places that smell like burnout.

Discussion Questions

  • How has your perspective on gathering changed over the last 12 sessions?
  • Who outside this group might need an invitation to the table next?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to send you back into everyday life with a slower presence, a listening posture, and the courage to initiate care instead of waiting for it.

Personal Practice

Host your own Percolate moment this week. Invite one person to coffee with no agenda beyond practicing presence.

Additional Reading

Commencement

Now go pour outward.

Twelve weeks of honest conversation, shared presence, and faithful attention were never the finish line. Take what was practiced here into the rest of your life. Keep the table open, and keep inviting people to it.
