“For God gave us a spirit not of fear but of power and love and self-control.”
~ 2 Timothy 1:7 (ESV)

Reflection

Suffering is part of life, and as a Christ follower, we know that God does not waste our experiences and uses them to grow us. Pain itself is not good, but God loves us and will help us endure it for something better. Sharing your struggles with the body of Christ will allow others to walk with you and help build your faith. God loves us and has given us a sound mind capable of discernment, which helps us endure suffering, overcome fear, build resilience, and grow as individuals.

Discussion Questions

  • What challenges have shaped you?
  • How can community relationships help you?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you how to trust him and persevere.

Personal Practice

Identify a challenge that is a growth opportunity.
