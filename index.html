Percolate is designed for small groups gathering over coffee, tea, or whatever brings you to the table. Each week explores a theme through scripture, reflection, and honest discussion. No perfect answers required — just show up and be real.

About Percolate

Percolate is a 12-week Bible study created by Angela Creason as part of her Master of Fine Arts thesis project. It explores how faith, authentic friendships, and face-to-face conversation can help us navigate anxiety, isolation, and the longing for genuine connection in a digitally saturated world.
