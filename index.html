Why Percolate exists

Percolate was designed as a soft but intentional container for honest table conversation. Each week pairs scripture, reflection, and personal practice so participants can move from guarded distance to embodied connection.

How it works

Built for small groups, coffee tables, and honest conversation.

Each week is structured to move from scripture into reflection, discussion, prayer, and embodied practice. It is thoughtful without becoming rigid.

Design logic

The color cycle becomes part of the emotional rhythm.

Angela’s repeating palette gives the study a visual cadence: calm, bright, urgent, grounded, then renewed again. The system becomes part of the experience.

Why it matters

Percolate treats presence as a form of care.

This isn’t content for passive scrolling. It is a designed invitation back into embodied faith, conversation, and relational attention.
