“Be still, and know that I am God...”
~ Psalm 46:10 (ESV)

Reflection

We rush from one thing to the next, finding ourselves exhausted. The Lord desires for us to know Him and be present. Presence is listening and trusting in the Lord, and not about inactivity. In the stillness, we become aware of his truths and how he cares for us. Take moments throughout the day to enjoy the stillness and calm. Set aside other distractions and focus on the conversation. Presence is about engaging in the moment to deepen relationships.

Discussion Questions

  • What are some ways to practice stillness?
  • What effort does it take to be present with others?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you when and how to be still and present.

Personal Practice

Practice being present daily.
