“Fear not, for I am with you; be not dismayed, for I am your God; I will strengthen you, I will help you, I will uphold you with my righteous right hand.”
~ Isaiah 41:10 (ESV)

Reflection

Anxiety can overwhelm us in our daily lives. Social media, world events, and decision overload through daily activities can amplify our fear. The Lord promises to be our strength and protector. "Fear not" means we have other choices. We can trust the Lord to walk us through the things that overwhelm us by surrendering our fears and the things we cannot control to his care. God is trustworthy and loves us more than we can imagine.

Discussion Questions

  • What do you do when you feel overwhelmed?
  • What does God's presence mean to you?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you how to trust him when you become overwhelmed.

Personal Practice

When you feel anxious, pause and intentionally evaluate where you are placing your trust.
