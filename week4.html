Week 4

Renew
Your Mind

Coffee Moment

☕

Set your phone down and allow this moment to be free from distractions.

“...Be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that by testing you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and acceptable and perfect.”
~ Romans 12:2 (ESV)

Reflection

Modern life bombards us with digital noise and constant stimulation that divides our attention, increases mental fatigue, and overwhelms us with pressure. The Lord is not surprised that we struggle with mental health, and scripture is full of ways to help us distinguish truth from lies, because “the truth will set you free” (John 8:32). If the truth is freedom, then errors weigh us down. Renewing your mind is an ongoing exercise in trusting the Lord.

Discussion Questions

  • What distracts you from being present?
  • How does distraction affect relationships?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you how to renew your mind.

Personal Practice

Intentionally choose a distraction-free moment each day this week.
