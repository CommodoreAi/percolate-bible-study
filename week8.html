“Bear one another's burdens, and so fulfill the law of Christ.”
~ Galatians 6:2 (ESV)

Reflection

Community is experiencing regular, ordinary interactions with people. Sharing our struggles and successes with others becomes a learning opportunity, allowing us to encourage and, at times, be encouraged by others. Loving others is not about fixing their problems, but about listening, caring, praying, and pointing each other back to the Lord as our strength. In both good and difficult times, we can be compassionate and find encouragement. Life with others is an opportunity for spiritual growth, one step at a time.

Discussion Questions

  • What does burden-sharing look like in today's world?
  • What does it look like to show love in everyday life?

Prayer

Ask the Lord to show you how to love people in the moment.

Personal Practice

Share a practical need with someone. Respond to a need with an act of care.
